THE GAME

We need your help to investigate the entity known as Michael and take him down. His preferred communication method is through social media - notably snapchat and instagram

How To Enter The Investigation:

1. Scan the snapcode to add michael on snapchat or follow michael on instagram by following "michaelwatching"

2. DM a selfie captioned "come find me" to be added to his list

3. Michael will send clues through the Story function, you will need to use this clues to find locations where a "michael sticker" can be found. These locations will lead you to information.

4. At the same time, Michael will be hunting you down, so be alert. He will catch you by taking a photo where your face is clearly visible. If you are caught, you'll be forced to work for him.

If You Are A Hunter:

1. There must be a 1.5 metre radius around targets

2. photographs and videos must be taken on campus only, and only during the set play sessions(?)

Terms and Conditions:

1.By sending the selfie captioned "come find me" to the snapchat or instagram accounts, you will be consenting to being photographed and videod for the duration of the investigation

3. You may not harm other players, physically, verbally or emotionally, during or outside the investigation.
